How Swachify promotes PLOGGING?

Plogging is a combination of two words: jogging and Swedish phrase for pick up, ‘plocka upp’.Plogging is an eco-friendly exercise through which people pick up trash while jogging or brisk walking as a way to clean up litter and also take care of their health.Our aim is to tell the world “littering is not acceptable”.Plogging, not only helps us to make the surroundings cleaner, but also helps you burn a lot of calories,relieve stress,grow confidence,self-esteem,supports heart health anti depression and helps to get away your winter blues . It combines the Fit India Movement and Swachh Bharat Abhiyan........
